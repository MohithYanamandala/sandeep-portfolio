ABOUT ME

I design clothes that move with you, not against you. My work blends clean tailoring with street comfort, so you can wear one look from meetings to late night conversations without changing who you are.

I work in small runs, focus on fabric first, and build outfits around how you actually live. The goal is simple: fewer pieces in your wardrobe, more outfits that feel like you on your best day.
